Born in Santa Monica and raised on the westside of Los Angeles, I grew up a curious and artsy kid.

Storytelling fascinated me at a young age.

I used to play make believe with my friends and siblings regularly and come up with enthralling stories for my toys - my imagination knew no boundaries. As I got older, I decided to refine my skills and started creative writing. I wrote short stories, songs, poetry, and screenplays. I never wanted to be famous, but I did want a personal achievement in the craft that I loved - one that no one could take away from me. I was able to accomplish that goal sooner that I anticipated.
