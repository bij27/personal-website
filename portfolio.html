Here are some projects that I worked on that I'm incredibly proud of.

I hope you enjoy watching them as much as I enjoyed making them!

Hear No Evil (2022)

Written & Directed by Bijan Jadhavji

Other roles: Director of Photography, Editor

This slasher horror takes place on a college campus and comes complete with a mysterious lurker and a scream queen.

Won "Best Short Film" and "Best Overall Film" in 2022 at the Moorpark Film Festival in Los Angeles.

Buddy (2022)

Written & Co-Directed by Bijan Jadhavji

Other roles: Director of Photography, Editor

This is a fun, quirky take on a sitcom with a bit of a horror twist! A lot of fun to make.

This film earned us "Best Use of Genre" in the 2022 48 Hour Film Festival in Los Angeles - a small but impressive feat for a low-budget crew.
